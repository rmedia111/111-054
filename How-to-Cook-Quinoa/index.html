How to Cook Quinoa

Cooking quinoa is quick and easy. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove its natural coating. Combine quinoa and water or broth in a pot with a 2:1 liquid-to-quinoa ratio. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed. Let it sit off the heat for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork. Use quinoa as a base for salads, bowls, or as a side dish.
